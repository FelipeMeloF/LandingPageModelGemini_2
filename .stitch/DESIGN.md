# Imobiliária Extremo Oriente -- Design System

## Brand Feel
Sofisticado, Confiável, Prático

## Color Direction
*   **Primary Accent:** Gold (`#D4AF37`) -- Represents prestige, elegance, quality, and the "East" heritage ("Extremo Oriente").
*   **Theme Mode:** DARK (Luxurious near-black `#0A0A0A` for main background, `#121212` for card backgrounds).
*   **Secondary/Text:** Curated dark grays (`#1F1F1F` to `#8F8F8F`) and warm ivory/cream (`#F4F4F0`) for text readability, avoiding stark contrasts for a smoother, premium aesthetic.

## Typography
*   **Headlines:** Domine -- A sturdy, highly elegant, readable traditional serif that brings heritage and high-end trust to the brand.
*   **Body:** DM Sans -- A warm, modern geometric sans-serif that ensures ease of reading and a high-tech modern app feeling.

## Shape
*   **Roundness:** Balanced, modern rounded corners (`ROUND_EIGHT`, `8px` / `0.5rem`).
*   **Containers:** Premium glassmorphism borders (`rgba(212, 175, 55, 0.1)`) and subtle dark gold radial glow backdrops to enhance visual depth.
